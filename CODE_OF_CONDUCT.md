# Code of Conduct

This project aims to be rigorous, welcoming, and useful to researchers,
engineers, and students working on reasoning systems.

## Our Standard

We expect contributors and maintainers to:

- be respectful in technical disagreement
- critique ideas, claims, and implementations rather than people
- keep discussion evidence-oriented and reproducible where possible
- make room for questions from contributors with different backgrounds
- avoid dismissive, hostile, or demeaning behavior

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable in project spaces:

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- personal attacks or sustained antagonism
- sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention
- doxxing or publishing private information without consent
- deliberately disruptive behavior in issues, merge requests, or discussions

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the repository
maintainers privately through the hosting platform rather than opening a public
issue. Include links, screenshots, or concrete context when possible.

Maintainers may remove content, pause discussions, or restrict participation
when needed to protect the project space.
